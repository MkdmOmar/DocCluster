Fresh off his turn fashioning hundreds of mini-doughnuts for the Met Gala last week, Mark Isreal, the owner of Doughnut Plant, turned to what was next on his calendar: Mother’s Day. He has created big yeast “Doughflowers” shaped like roses and available in a choice of glazes: strawberry, made from fresh berries; rose, richly perfumed with rose water and crushed edible petals; and Italian blood orange, sweet and tart with orange blossom water.
